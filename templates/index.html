AI-Powered Medical Diagnosis System

An advanced machine learning-based medical diagnosis system that analyzes patient symptoms to predict diseases and provide comprehensive treatment recommendations including medications, precautions, diet plans, and workout routines.

Symptom Analysis

Advanced autocomplete system with 133 symptoms for accurate disease prediction. Real-time suggestions and multi-symptom selection capabilities.

Machine Learning

Trained model using various models for precise disease classification and prediction with high accuracy.

Treatment Recommendations

Comprehensive treatment plans including primary and alternate antibiotics, detailed medication descriptions, and usage guidelines.

Health Management

Complete health management with diet plans, workout routines, and precautionary measures tailored to specific diseases.

Technology Stack

Backend: Python Flask

ML: Scikit-learn

Frontend: Bootstrap 5

Data: Pandas, NumPy
